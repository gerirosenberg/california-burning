California Burning

(2008-2016)

Click each circle for details.

Best viewed on desktop.

This map shows the total amount of acres burned per year in each California county due to wildfires. More than half of the 20 most destructive wildfires in recorded California history (as of February 2019) have occurred since 2008. At the top of the page, you can filter the data by which counties had the most acres burned in the selected year. This data does not discriminate by cause.

Source: CAL FIRE Icons: The Noun Project
